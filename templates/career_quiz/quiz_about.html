About the Career Discovery Quiz

Learn how the quiz works and what to expect.

The Career Discovery Quiz helps you find majors and careers that fit your interests and personality. No sign-up required—just answer a few questions and get personalized suggestions.

Short vs full quiz

You can take a short quiz (10 questions, about 3 minutes) for a quick snapshot, or the full quiz (25 questions, about 6–7 minutes) for more accurate, research-backed results. Both use the same style of questions; the full quiz adds more on personality, teamwork, and job preferences.

Two ways to take it

  • I have a major in mind — Pick your major first, then take the quiz. You’ll get job suggestions that fit your degree and your personality.
  • I’m not sure what I want — Take the quiz without choosing a major. We’ll suggest majors and job categories that fit you, plus sample careers to explore.

What you’ll get

After you finish, you’ll see your top career categories, a short summary of your results, and concrete career suggestions—either filtered by your major or as a broader list of majors and jobs to explore. You can use this to narrow down options, choose a major, or get ideas for next steps.

Short quiz: ~3 min · Full quiz: ~6–7 min · Free, no account needed
